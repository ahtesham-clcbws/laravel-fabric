# 🕸️ Nexus Graph: Visualizing Architecture

The **Nexus Graph** is a powerful visualization tool within the Fabric Lab that maps out your application's data architecture.

## Visualizing Complexity

The `fabric:graph` command generates a Mermaid-compatible or JSON-based map of your application. When viewed in the Fabric Lab, this becomes an interactive graph where you can:

- **Trace Relationships**: See how models are interconnected across your entire ecosystem.
- **Spot Bottlenecks**: Identify "God Models" with too many relationships.
- **Audit Data Flow**: Understand how data moves from one resource to another.

## Features
- **Dynamic Filtering**: Focus on specific groups of models or relationships.
- **Auto-Layout**: Intelligently arranges models based on relationship density.
- **Exportable**: Export your architecture as a high-resolution image for documentation.

---

> [!TIP]
> The Nexus Graph is updated in real-time as you forge new resources or update your database schema.
